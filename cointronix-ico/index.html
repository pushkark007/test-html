Securities Exchange


Cointronix will seek to be a fully regulated security exchange in Asia, likely in Singapore, and in the US. Companies that have been approved by local regulators to offer security tokens either one of the two jurisdictions will be allowed to be fast tracked into a dual listing to the second exchange at minimal cost, provided they have also complied with the securities laws in the second jurisdiction. The dual listing in an exchange in Asia and the US will serve to increase the company’s global brand and promote greater liquidity through broader global investor interest for the company’s security tokens as both listed security tokens will be fungible with each other.

Securities Trading

With Cointronix


Unlike traditional securities exchange, Cointronix will make it much easier and cheaper for investors/traders to become a member of the exchange. For example, the membership fee for the Chicago Mercantile Exchange cost around US$586,000 and for Singapore Exchange a securities membership requires the member to have a base capital of S$1 million (~US$735,000). This will allow some investors that don’t need the extra service provided by brokers to access the securities market directly and help reduce or eliminate the brokerage cost.

For new security tokens looking to list with us we will also help them to develop smart contracts that can help reduce the cost for issuers and investors. For example, the management of the ownership of securities can be done using smart contracts and the data can be stored securely on a distributed ledger.
