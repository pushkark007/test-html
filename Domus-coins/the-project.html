The Idea: a Real Estate Cryptocurrency

What if...

...people could partecipate on a real estate purchase with no need of lawyers or authorities to be registered but at the same keeping a universally recognized right on the purchase?

What if huge real estate projects can be owned by ordinary people like you and not only by sophisticated and inaccessibile groups?

What if ordinary people can make profit from real estate even with a small amount of money ?

The Blockchain makes it possible

Imagine the Blockchain as a universal register of transactions. Nobody can change what's written on it and people from around the world can read its content.

If somebody ever tries to change what's on the blockchain, everybody will immediately notice the counterfeit attempt and therefore the change will be invalidated.

With this premise, when a participant gives us money, we will store this transaction on the blockchain returning an amount of our real estate coins to the participant. The amount of coins represents the amount that the participant transferred to us and the blockchain keeps a universal track of such an exchange.

The participant can now decide to keep the coins or sell them at some time. What we are making now is a proper real estate cryptocurrency.

The Project

How it works

Everybody, even you, can partecipate to this project and it is quite easy.

First of all you don't need a big quantity of money, you can partecipate even with 10 USD or whatever you feel comfortable with.


Let's explain with an example:

  • You decide to partecipate with 10 USD which you transfer to us through one of the payment methods we offer.

  • Once we receive the 10 USD, we wil send to you 10 DOCS (Domus Coins), the transaction is public and stored on the BlockChain although the names are anonymous.

  • You will have a private key (a sort of password) and only you will be able to manage the coins.

  • We will allocate your 10 USD to buy our next property.

  • Now you are free to keep your 10 DOCS or sell to whoever you want.

Partecipate Now

What's cool about this project?


The cool thing behind the project is that you will enjoy all the benefits of owning a property without spending the amount of money that you would normally need.

From a property there are usually two types of profit that you can make. In our case you have three + 1. Let's analyze the types of profit that you will have :


  • Renting. When you own a property it's likely that you rent it out and take a profit out of it. With our project this is exactly what happens: we will rent the property and pay you back Domus Coins which, once again, you can decide to keep or sell.

  • Selling. When you sell a property you, hopefully, make a profit. Again, this happens with us: when we sell a property we share the profit with you in the form of Domus Coins.

  • Domus Coins appreciation. Domus Coins (DOCS) is the crypto-currency we use to pay back you and the other founders. DOCS are always backed by real value, that is, the properties owned by Domus Coins. This means that the value of DOCS will appreciate together with the value of the properties. Therefore you will be able to sell DOCS at an higher value after some time.

  • Hassle-free. Last but not least you don't have to worry about bureaucracy, as our team will take care of all the procedures.


Furthermore, exactly as with a real property, you will be able to stay in any property of the network. You will be able to do this by paying a rental fee in DOCS.

Inflation

Inflation is kept under control, in fact DOCS will be crafted at market value rate only when new capitals are brought into the company.

Profit Sharing

Profits come from the rentals and sales of the properties. They are shared proportionally with the founders according to the amount of DOCS that they own.

Coins Buyback

Domus Coins does not buy back its own coins, but it is possible to exchange them on third party exchangers.

Transparency & Audits

Every 6 months we have external auditors that will certify the value of the coins in circulation as well as the value of the company. So you can be safe that the company is running succesfully.
